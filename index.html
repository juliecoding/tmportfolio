Outreach

Since Fall 2022, I've been a technical instructor for Tech-Moms, a non-profit organization that teaches women basic coding and exposes them to the many kinds of job opportunities in tech. Hundreds of women have come through the program and have improved their tech skills and career prospects. The courses are not designed to be a bootcamp, but rather a career exploration program with a coding component.

Teaching the class is one of the most meaningful parts of my week. My favorite part is watching my students gain greater confidence and discover that they can "do" tech. It's not an unapproachable, incomprehensible discipline; it's a dynamic part of today's world, one they can thrive in.

If you want to learn more about our program or even become a participant, please visit tech-moms.org

Work

I'm a full-stack Senior Software Engineer at Splice, whose library of royalty-free samples is used by top music producers to write songs. We work in Go, MySQL, GraphQL, and Javascript. Our infrastructure runs on Amazon Web Services. My projects have involved asset ingestion, decomposition, and downloads. Splice is an awesome place to work because the business-to-consumer model makes me feel like I'm making a difference in software that means a lot to people. I love that we further producers' artistic endeavors and fuel their creativity.
